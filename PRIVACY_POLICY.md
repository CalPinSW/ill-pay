# Privacy Policy for I'll Pay

**Last Updated:** January 23, 2026

## Introduction

I'll Pay ("we", "our", or "the app") is a receipt splitting application that helps users share bills with friends. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Username and display name (for identification)
- Profile picture (optional)

### Receipt Data
- Photos of receipts you upload
- Extracted receipt information (restaurant name, items, prices, tax, tip)
- Receipt sharing and participation data

### Usage Data
- App interactions and feature usage
- Device information (for troubleshooting)

## How We Use Your Information

- **Authentication**: To verify your identity and secure your account
- **Receipt Processing**: To extract and display receipt information
- **Bill Splitting**: To calculate and track who owes what
- **Social Features**: To enable sharing receipts with friends

## Data Storage

- All data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS)
- Receipt images are stored in secure cloud storage
- Passwords are never stored in plain text

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your receipt data for marketing purposes

We **do** share:
- Receipt data with users you explicitly invite to split a bill
- Aggregate, anonymized usage statistics (for app improvement)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data
- Opt out of optional data collection

## Data Retention

- Active account data is retained while your account exists
- Deleted receipts are permanently removed within 30 days
- Account deletion removes all associated data

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication (Supabase Auth)
- Row-level security on all database tables

## Children's Privacy

I'll Pay is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.

## Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact:
- Email: privacy@illpay.app

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **OpenAI**: Receipt parsing (images processed but not stored by OpenAI)
- **Expo**: App distribution and updates

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs their use of data.
